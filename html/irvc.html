IRVC — University of California, Irvine Herbarium

The UC Irvine Herbarium is administered as part of the UC Irvine Arboretum. It holds specimens from western North America, especially Orange County, the rest of southern California, and Baja California, Mexico. Size of collection: 25,000 vascular plant specimens, 5000 lichens and algae.

Director: Peter Bowler (pabowler at uci.edu)
